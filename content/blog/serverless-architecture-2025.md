## FAQ

### What is serverless architecture?
A cloud model where the provider dynamically manages the allocation of machine resources. The "serverless" name comes from the fact that the business doesn't manage servers.

### Is serverless more expensive?
For most startups and mid-market companies, serverless is significantly cheaper because you don't pay for "idle" server time.

### How does serverless affect web performance?
With modern "Edge Functions," serverless can be even faster than traditional servers by running code closer to the user, a key part of our [LINK: technical-seo-audit-2025].
