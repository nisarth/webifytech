## FAQ

### What is psychographic profiling?
Grouping people based on their psychological attributes like personality, values, interests, and lifestyles.

### How do I get psychographic data?
Through user interviews, surveys, and analyzing which types of [LINK: social-commerce-trends-2025] content your audience engages with most.
