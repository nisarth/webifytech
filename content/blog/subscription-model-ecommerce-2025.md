## The Three Main Models
1. **Replenishment**: Automated re-orders of consumables (e.g., razor blades, pet food).
2. **Curation**: Sending a unique selection of items each month (e.g., wine, beauty products).
3. **Access**: Paying for exclusive perks, discounts, or early launches (as seen in our [LINK: portfolio]).

## Overcoming the Churn Challenge
To keep subscribers, you need:
- **Easy Management**: A user-friendly portal to pause, skip, or cancel.
- **Value Consistency**: Ensuring the convenience or savings remain relevant over time.
- **Performance UX**: A fast, seamless checkout process as outlined in our [LINK: optimising-checkout-conversion-2025].
