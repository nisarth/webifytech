## FAQ

### What is the biggest e-commerce threat in 2025?
"Supply Chain" attacks, where hackers compromise a small tool or script you use on your site (like a chat widget) to steal data.

### How does security affect conversion?
Trust signals (like 'Secure Checkout' badges) and a site that never crashes or redirects dramatically improve conversion, as noted in our [LINK: conversion-rate-optimization-checklist-2025].
