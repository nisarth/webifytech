## FAQ

### Is Google Search Console really free?
Yes. It is a free tool provided by Google to help website owners monitor and optimize their presence in Google Search results.

### What is the difference between GSC and Google Analytics?
GSC focuses on "Search Performance" (how users find you on Google, indexation, technical errors). Google Analytics focuses on "User Behavior" (what users do once they arrive on your site, conversion rates, traffic sources beyond search). You need both for a successful [LINK: services] strategy.

### How often should I check my Search Console?
We recommend a weekly check for performance trends and a monthly deep-dive into your [LINK: technical-seo-audit-2025] and indexation reports. If you receive a "Security" or "Manual Action" email, check it immediately.

### Why is my page "Crawled - currently not indexed"?
This means Google has visited the page but decided not to add it to the index yet. This can be due to "thin content," technical errors (like a canonical loop), or because the site hasn't established enough topical authority yet. Our current [LINK: content-auditing-for-seo] sprint is designed specifically to fix this.

### Can GSC help me with International SEO?
Yes! As detailed in our [LINK: international-seo-guide-2025], GSC allows you to track performance by country and monitor the health of your Hreflang tags, which are essential for targeting multiple languages and regions.
