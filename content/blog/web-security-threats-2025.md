## The Emerging Threats of 2025
- **AI-Driven Phishing**: Attacks that are indistinguishable from legitimate communication.
- **Bot-Based Volumetric Attacks**: Automated attempts to overwhelm your API or checkout.
- **Third-Party Script Vulnerabilities**: Exploiting small libraries you include on your site.

## How to Protect Your Assets
1. **Content Security Policy (CSP)**: Control exactly which scripts can run on your site.
2. **Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA)**: A must-have for any admin access.
