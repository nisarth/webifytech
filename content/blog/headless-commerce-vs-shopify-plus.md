## Shopify Plus: The Convenience King
Shopify Plus is the "all-in-one" solution for brands that want to move fast without managing their own complex infrastructure.
- **Pros**: Rapid deployment, massive library of apps, and world-class reliability.
- **Cons**: Limited checkout customization and transaction fees that can become significant at scale.

## Headless Commerce: The Ultimate Freedom
Headless decouples the frontend from the backend, allowing you to use a custom [LINK: headless-cms-benefits-2025] and a specialized commerce engine.
- **Pros**: Total design freedom, extreme [LINK: ssr-vs-ssg-2025-debate] performance, and no "template" restrictions.
- **Cons**: Higher initial development cost and more "moving parts" to maintain.
