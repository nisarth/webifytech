## 1. What is Shopify Plus in 2025?
Shopify Plus is the enterprise version of Shopify. It offers all the convenience of a SaaS platform but with higher limits, more support, and advanced features.
- **Shopify Functions**: Allowing for custom logic in discounts, shipping, and payments without the need for complex "hacks."
- **Wholesale/B2B Excellence**: A dedicated platform for managing high-volume business customers alongside your retail store.
- **Multipass & SSO**: Securely managing user data across multiple subdomains or regional stores. (See [LINK: ecommerce-security-checklist-2025]).
- **The Verdict**: Best for brands that want "Professional Convenience"—the power of an enterprise platform with the maintenance of a SaaS.

---

## 2. What is Headless Commerce in 2025?
Headless commerce is an architectural choice where you use a backend (like Shopify, BigCommerce, or a [LINK: scalable APIs 2025] custom DB) but build your own bespoke frontend (the "head") using frameworks like Next.js.
- **Unmatched Speed**: Because the frontend is a standalone [LINK: serverless-architecture-2025] app, it can achieve [LINK: technical-seo-audit-2025] scores that are impossible on a monolithic platform.
- **Total Design Freedom**: No themes. No liquid layouts. Just pure, brand-perfect [LINK: ux-design-principles-2025].
- **Infinite Personalization**: As detailed in [LINK: ecommerce-personalization-2025], headless allows you to swap content and layouts in real-time based on the user's intent.
- **The Verdict**: Best for brands that want "Complete Control"—those who see their digital experience as their primary competitive advantage.

## FAQ

### Can I use Shopify as the backend for a headless store?
Yes! This is one of the most popular setups in 2025. You use Shopify Plus for all the "Cart and Checkout" logic but build a custom Next.js frontend for the experience. (Learn more in our [LINK: complete-guide-web-development-2025]).

### Is headless commerce harder to manage?
For your marketing team, no. If we use a [LINK: headless-cms-benefits-2025] like Payload or Contentful, the editing experience is often better than Shopify’s native editor. For the development team, yes—it requires high-level [LINK: services] from an expert agency like WebifyTech.

### Does headless help with international SEO?
Massively. As detailed in our [LINK: international-seo-guide-2025], headless allows for totally localized subdirectories and hreflang control that can be difficult to manage on a monolithic platform.

### What is the biggest risk with Headless?
"Integration Complexity." If you don't use a partner who understands [LINK: scalable APIs 2025], your frontend can become disconnected from your backend. This is why we specialize in robust, [LINK: serverless-architecture-2025] commerce.

### When should I move from Shopify to Shopify Plus?
Typically when you hit $1M+ in ARR, or when you need advanced features like wholesale channels, custom checkout logic, or dedicated account support. See our [LINK: shopify-vs-custom-ecommerce-2025] for a detailed breakdown.
