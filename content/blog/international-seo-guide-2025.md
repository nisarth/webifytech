## FAQ

### Should I use Google Translate for my international site?
No! Machine translation often misses cultural context and creates "unnatural" text that Google penalizes. Always use native editors.

### Is International SEO expensive?
It's an investment in a much larger market. The ROI of ranking in a new country can be 10x higher than trying to squeeze more traffic out of a saturated local market.
