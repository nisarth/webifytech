## Step 1: Crawl Accessibility and Bot Guidance
If search bots can't get in, they can't rank you.
- **Robots.txt Analysis**: Ensure your most important pages (like [LINK: services] and [LINK: portfolio]) aren't accidentally blocked.
- **XML Sitemap Health**: Your sitemap should be clean, concise, and include only 200-OK pages with high value.
- **Internal Link Architecture**: Using a logical hierarchy to distribute "link juice" from your high-authority [LINK: complete-guide-web-development-2025] pillars to your product and service pages.

## Step 2: Site Speed and Core Web Vitals (CWV)
In 2025, speed is a comparative metric. You don't just need to be fast; you need to be faster than your competitors.
- **LCP (Largest Contentful Paint)**: How long it takes for the main content to load. (Goal: < 2.5 seconds).
- **INP (Interaction to Next Paint)**: The 2024 replacement for FID, measuring how fast the site responds to user clicks. (See [LINK: micro-animations-ux-2025] for performance tips).
- **Asset Optimization**: Using Next-Gen formats (WebP/AVIF) and [LINK: serverless-architecture-2025] for lightning-fast delivery.

## FAQ

### How long does a technical audit take to perform?
A comprehensive audit typically takes 10-15 business days to complete, depending on the size and complexity of the site. Implementing the findings can take anywhere from a few days to several weeks.

### Will a technical audit fix my rankings immediately?
Technical fixes often result in a "correction"—you may see a surge in indexation and improved speed scores within 7-10 days. The full ranking impact usually manifests over 4-8 weeks as Google re-crawls and re-evaluates the improved structure.

### Can I do a technical audit myself?
While you can use tools like Lighthouse, a "Professional Audit" requires a deep understanding of log files, server configurations, and search intent. We recommend the experts at WebifyTech handle the heavy lifting for your [LINK: services].

### Does "Serverless" help with SEO?
Yes! As detailed in our [LINK: serverless-architecture-2025] guide, serverless platforms like Vercel or AWS Lambda provide superior speed and reliability compared to traditional hosting, which are key signals to search engines.

### What is "Crawl Budget"?
Crawl budget is the number of pages search engines will crawl on your site on a given day. If your site is full of duplicate content or slow-loading pages (see [LINK: ecommerce SEO strategy]), the bot might leave before finding your most important content.
