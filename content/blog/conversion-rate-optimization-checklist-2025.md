## FAQ

### What is a good conversion rate?
It varies, but for B2B [LINK: resources], 2-5% is healthy. For e-commerce, 1-3% is typical.

### How often should I run CRO tests?
CRO is an ongoing process. We recommend running at least one major A/B test per month to stay ahead of the curve.

### Does UX design affect CRO?
UX is the foundation of CRO. If the user can't navigate easily, they will never convert, as explained in our [LINK: ux-design-principles-2025].
