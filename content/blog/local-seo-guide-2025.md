## FAQ

### How long does it take for Local SEO to show results?
For a new Google Business Profile, it can take 30-90 days to start appearing in the "Local Map Pack." For established businesses, improvements in rankings from local content and citation cleanup can be seen in as little as 4-6 weeks.

### Do I need a physical office for Local SEO?
While a physical office is the strongest signal, "Service Area Businesses" (like plumbers or mobile dog groomers) can still rank well by defining their service radius correctly in GBP. Learn more in our [LINK: services] section.

### Is Local SEO different from traditional organic SEO?
Yes! Traditional SEO focuses on "Broad Relevance" across the web. Local SEO focuses on "Geographic Relevance" and rely heavily on the Google Maps algorithm. You need both to succeed. (See [LINK: ecommerce SEO strategy] for the national perspective).

### How does voice search impact local SEO?
Voice search is almost always local. Users typically ask "Where is..." or "What's the best... near me." Having clear, structured [LINK: contact] data and FAQ schema is essential for being the voice answer. See [LINK: voice-search-optimization-2025] for more.

### Should I use my city name in my URL?
For local service pages, yes (e.g., `webifytech.com/web-design-london`). For your homepage or general [LINK: about] page, it’s usually better to keep it broad to build national authority while using sub-pages to target specific regions.
