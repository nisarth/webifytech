## The Rise of Answer Engine Optimization (AEO)
AEO is the practice of structuring your content so that it is easily understood, verified, and cited by Large Language Models (LLMs). Unlike traditional SEO, which focuses on keyword density and backlinks, AEO focuses on:
- **Conceptual Clarity**: Does the AI understand exactly what you are offering?
- **Data Verifiability**: Can the AI cross-reference your claims with other high-authority sources?
- **Entity Association**: Is your brand seen as an "Authority" in a specific topic cluster?

## FAQ

### Will AI search engines kill website traffic?
For "informational" sites that provide simple facts, traffic may decrease as users get their answers directly in the SERP. However, for "transactional" and "expert" sites (like service providers and high-end brands), AI search can actually drive *higher-quality* traffic from users who have been "pre-qualified" by the AI's answer.

### What is Schema Markup, and why does it matter for AI?
Schema Markup is a form of structured data that tells search engines exactly what the elements on your page are (e.g., "This is a price," "This is a physical location," "This is a service"). AI uses this data to build its "Knowledge Graph" and provide accurate answers.

### How often should I update my content for AI search?
In the fast-moving world of 2025, regular updates are essential. We recommend a [LINK: content-auditing-for-seo] every quarter to ensure your facts are current, your links are healthy, and your entity authority remains strong.

### Can AI help me write my SEO content?
Yes, but with caution. AI is a great tool for outlining and research, but it lacks the "Experience" required for top-tier rankings. The winning formula is a human-led strategy with AI assistance. See [LINK: ai-content-vs-human-writing-2025] for more.

### How do I track my performance in AI search?
Traditional rank tracking is becoming less effective. In 2025, we focus on "Citation Tracking"—monitoring how often your brand is mentioned as an authoritative source in AI-generated answers and through specialized tools like GSC's Generative Performance report.
