## The Science of Dark Mode
- **Reduced Eye Strain**: Especially in low-light environments, dark themes reduce the "glare" that causes digital fatigue.
- **Improved Focus**: Contrast is often higher in dark mode, making it easier for users to focus on primary [LINK: resources].
- **Battery Efficiency**: On OLED screens (common in 2025), black pixels are literally turned off, saving up to 30% battery.

## FAQ

### Should every website have dark mode?
If your users spend more than a few minutes on your site, yes. It significantly improves the "Time on Site" metric in our [LINK: ux-design-principles-2025].

### Is dark mode better for accessibility?
For some users with light sensitivity, yes. However, it's important to always provide a high-contrast light mode alternative.

### How do I trigger dark mode automatically?
We use a small script that detects the user's OS preference (prefers-color-scheme) and instantly applies the correct theme.
