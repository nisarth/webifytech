## FAQ

### What is Semantic SEO?
Optimizing content for the meaning behind search queries rather than just specific keyword matches.

### How do I build a topic cluster?
Start with a "Pillar Page" about a broad topic, then create "Cluster Content" that dives into specific sub-topics, all linking back to the pillar.
