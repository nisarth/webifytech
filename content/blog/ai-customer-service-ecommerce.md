## The Shift from Chatbots to AI Agents

Early chatbots were frustrating and limited. In 2025, we use Conversational AI agents powered by Large Language Models (LLMs). These agents can:
- **Understand Context**: They don't just look for keywords; they understand the nuance of a customer's question.
- **Process Transactions**: An AI agent can track an order, process a return, or update a shipping address without human intervention.
- **Personalize Answers**: By connecting to your CRM, AI can provide answers based on the customer's specific purchase history.
