# Site Migration SEO Checklist: Moving Without Losing Traffic in 2025

Moving your website is like moving your business's physical headquarters—if you don't update your address correctly, your customers will get lost. A **site migration SEO checklist** is your insurance policy against a sudden drop in rankings. Whether you are changing your domain or moving to a modern [LINK: headless-commerce-vs-shopify-plus] platform, precision is everything.

## The Pre-Migration Phase
- [ ] **Full Site Crawl**: Create a map of every current URL.
- [ ] **Benchmark Data**: Record your current rankings and organic traffic.
- [ ] **Staging Environment**: Build the new site on a private server first. Check our [LINK: services] for managed staging.

## The Technical Move
1. **301 Mapping**: Map every old URL to its most relevant new counterpart.
2. **Internal Link Audit**: Update all links in your [LINK: content-auditing-for-seo] to point to the new structure.
3. **Canonical Tag Update**: Ensure search engines know where the "source of truth" is now.

## FAQ

### How long does an SEO migration take?
The planning usually takes 2-4 weeks, the move happens in hours, and the post-migration monitoring lasts 30 days.

### Will I lose traffic?
A small, temporary dip is normal as Google re-crawls, but with a perfect redirect strategy, you should see a net gain within a month.

### Do I need to tell Google I'm moving?
Yes! Use the "Change of Address" tool in Google Search Console, as discussed in our [LINK: google-search-console-guide-2025].
