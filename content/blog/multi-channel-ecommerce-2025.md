## FAQ

### Is it hard to manage multiple channels?
It is if you do it manually. In 2025, we use automation and [LINK: scalable APIs 2025] to ensure that 90% of the cross-channel syncing is invisible and error-free.

### Which channel is most important?
Your own website (the "Canonical" store) is always most important because you own the customer data and the relationship. Everything else—social, marketplaces—should act as a "funnel" back to your own platform. (See [LINK: about]).

### Does multi-channel affect my SEO?
Yes! Consistent branding and NAP (Name, Address, Phone) across all channels build "Entity Authority" in [LINK: ai-powered-search-seo-2025]. Furthermore, multi-channel presence increases your brand's "Digital Footprint," which Google sees as a trust signal.

### Should I use a third-party syncing app?
For Shopify, apps like Linkpop or Stocky are great. For custom enterprise builds, we typically recommend a [LINK: serverless-architecture-2025] middleware that we build specifically for your [LINK: services].
