## The Elements of Quiet UI
- **Generous Whitespace**: Giving your content (and your users) room to breathe.
- **High-End Typography**: Using custom typefaces as the primary design element.
- **Subtle [LINK: micro-animations-ux-2025]**: Movement that feels natural and functional, not distracting.
- **Curated Color Palettes**: Using professional, harmonious tones instead of generic primaries.
