## Why Micro-Animations Matter
1. **Visual Feedback**: Confirming to the user that their action was successful.
2. **Reduced Cognitive Load**: Smoothly transitioning between states helps the brain track changes.
3. **Brand Personality**: High-end animations (like those in our [LINK: portfolio]) signal a level of craft and attention to detail.

## Best Practices for 2025
- **Less is More**: Never animate just for the sake of it.
- **Speed is Key**: Transitions should be fast (typically 200ms - 400ms) to avoid feeling sluggish.
- **Accessibility**: Always respect the user's "Reduced Motion" settings.

## FAQ

### What are micro-animations?
Small, functional animations that provide feedback or guide users within a digital interface.

### Do animations slow down my site?
Not if they are built with modern CSS or lightweight libraries like **Framer Motion**, as we use at WebifyTech.

### Should every button be animated?
No. Focus on the most important interactions where feedback is most valuable, as discussed in our [LINK: ux-design-principles-2025].
