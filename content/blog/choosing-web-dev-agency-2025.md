## 1. Look Beyond the Visuals: Technical Depth

## 2. Evaluate the Portfolio for "Real-World" Results
A portfolio should demonstrate more than aesthetic variety; it should show business impact.
- **Performance Scores**: Ask to see Lighthouse scores for their [LINK: portfolio] sites. In 2025, anything under 90/100 is unacceptable.
- **Scalability**: Have they built sites that handle high traffic? (See [LINK: serverless-architecture-2025]).
- **UX Maturity**: Look for [LINK: micro-animations-ux-2025] and [LINK: 2025-web-design-minimalist-luxury] aesthetics that enhance rather than distract.
