## The Three Pillars of Micro-Copy
1. **Clarity Over Cleverness**: Always tell the user exactly what will happen next.
2. **Contextual Empathy**: Addressing the user's specific worry (e.g., "No credit card required").
3. **Brand Voice**: Even an error message is an opportunity to show your brand's personality, as seen in our [LINK: portfolio].

## FAQ

### What is micro-copy?
The brief pieces of text on a user interface that help a visitor perform an action.

### Does micro-copy affect SEO?
While it isn't a primary keyword driver, it improves "Time on Site" and "Reduced Bounce Rate," which are major ranking signals.

### Should micro-copy be funny?
Only if it matches your brand's [LINK: psychographic-profiling-web-design]. For a law firm, stick to clarity. For a creative agency, have some fun!
